INAB|CERTH is co-leading two ELIXIR Tasks as commissioned services:

  • Tools Platform Task 4 “Software Best Practices”
  • Training Platform Task 2 “Gap analysis, training materials development and training delivery”

Additionally, we are co-leading three ELIXIR Focus Groups:

  • Machine Learning (AI) The ELIXIR Machine Learning Focus Group was initiated in October 2019, in order to capture the emerging need in Machine Learning expertise across the network.
  • Research Data Alliance (RDA) Activities This Focus Groups acts as the liaison between ELIXIR and RDA.
  • ELIXIR FAIR Training This Focus Group aims to provide guidelines for making training material FAIR, as well as producing material on the FAIR principles.
