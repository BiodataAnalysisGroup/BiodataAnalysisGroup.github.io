Biodata Analysis Group

Doing a little bit of bioinformatics, a dash of machine learning, and a lot of Open Science

Who We Are

The Biodata Analysis Group (also known as the Bioinformatics Lab) at the Institute of Applied Biosciences (INAB / CERTH) is active in Bioinformatics research, focusing on the design of new algorithms and pipelines.

What We Do

We are a group of enthusiastic researchers, active in the following key fields:

  • Machine Learning and Data Mining
  • Multi-omics data integration
  • Training for Life Sciences
